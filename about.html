About Us

About Us

Who we are

The ace company for TMT bars and structural steel — Savani Steel has built an extensive inventory, on-time delivery, genuine products, and economical rates that have helped form a brand name across the industry. We bring vast experience and knowledge in structural steel and TMT bars, wherever your project requires them.

We have geographically diversified our products, quality-checking everything in our in-house facility and getting it certified by a third-party agency. Tie-ups with multinational TMT and structural steel manufacturers let us offer nothing but the best quality — and our large warehouse means even large-scale orders ship cost-effectively and fast. We hold ourselves to high standards on the workshop floor, which is how we've delivered products across industries worldwide.

Purpose

Our Mission

To provide the aptest product for a client's requirement at the most affordable rate in the market. Understanding what a client needs, then suggesting the right product, is our USP — and it's how we build lasting relationships. We constantly upgrade our product management, solutions, and customer service, and hold every employee to the same quality standards: high-quality products, minimalistic but effective packaging, on-time delivery, reasonable rates, and support that continues after the sale.

Direction

Our Vision

We always keep our customers' needs and budget in mind before suggesting a product or solution, with a wide range of steel and tubes across measurements to match what a project actually requires. We aim to be prompt with solutions that favour the client on quality as well as cost — because holistic customer satisfaction is what matters at the end of the day.
